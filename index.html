About me

Since the age of sixteen, Youzhi Hu has been an avid programmer. He first started by modding TES games in Creation Kit that would allow him to grasp basic understanding of how computer application works, and his love of the potential applications of programming has been growing ever since.

The first programming language he started to use was Python. Although he attended the University of Sydney, where he majored in social science but realized that Python may come in handy when it comes to data analysis. Big Data and data mining was hot at that time, so he decided to pursue a Master's degree in Information Technology.

After obtaining his Master's degree in IT, Youzhi began working at HikVision Australia, where he started as a technical engineer. Afterwards, he decided to become a web developer and joined the coding bootcamp.
