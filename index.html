About me

Since the age of sixteen, I have been an avid programmer. I first started by modding TES games in Creation Kit that would allow me to grasp basic understanding of how computer application works, and my love of the potential applications of programming has been growing ever since.

The first programming language I started to use was Python. Although I attended the University of Sydney, where I majored in social science but realized that Python may come in handy for data analysis. I graduated when Big Data and data mining was booming, so I decided to pursue a Master's degree in Information Technology.

After obtaining my Master's degree in IT, I began working at HikVision Australia, where my primary focus was using data manipulation techniques to increace effecinecy of workflow. Afterwards, I have joined the coding bootcamp and learned how to become a good web developer.
